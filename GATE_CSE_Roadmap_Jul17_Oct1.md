# GATE CSE Roadmap — 17 July se 1 October (Phase 1: Foundation Building)

> **Assumption jo maine liya hai:** Aap fresh start kar rahe hain, aur full-time (student/dropper) prep kar sakte hain — isliye daily ~9-10 ghante ka schedule diya hai (study + practice + revision sab milाकर). Agar aap job/college ke saath kar rahe hain, to niche "Kam Time Version" section me halka schedule bhi diya hai — usse follow karein.
>
> Ye Phase 1 hai (77 din). Isme cover hoga: **Programming & DS, Discrete Math, Digital Logic, Algorithms, Computer Organization, Engineering Mathematics, Operating Systems, DBMS (partial)**.
> Baaki subjects — **CN, TOC, Compiler Design, Software Engineering** — Phase 2 me (Oct ke baad) cover honge, kyunki GATE exam Feb 2027 me hoga to abhi time hai.

## 4. Resources (Generic Suggestions — jo widely use hote hain)
- **Video Lectures:** NPTEL (official GATE syllabus ke hisaab se), any well-rated YouTube GATE CSE channel
- **Standard Books:** DS/Algo — Data Structures book of your choice + CLRS (reference only, poora mat padho); CO — Morris Mano; OS — Galvin; DBMS — Navathe/Korth; Discrete Math — Kenneth Rosen; Digital Logic — Morris Mano
- **PYQ + Test Series:** GATE Overflow (free PYQ + discussions), any GATE-focused test series (Made Easy / Ace Academy jaisa)
- **Formula/Notes:** Apna khud ka short notes banao — ye sabse zaroori hai revision ke liye

## 5. Important Tips
1. **Notes khud banao** — copy-paste mat karo, apni bhasha me likho, revision me time bachega.
2. **Har weekly test ke baad** — galat questions ka ek "Mistake Log" banao aur usse dobara dekho.
3. **PYQ solve karna** — sirf reading se kaam nahi chalega, GATE me application-based questions aate hain.
4. **Ek din break lena** — agar poore 11 weeks bina break ke thak jaate ho, to kisi bhi Day-7 ko half-day off le sakte ho, bas usse compensate karna Day-6 me thoda extra kaam karke.
5. **Health first** — neend, khana, exercise skip mat karo, warna consistency toot jaayegi.
6. **Phase 2 (Oct ke baad):** Computer Networks, Theory of Computation, Compiler Design, Software Engineering + poora syllabus ke multiple revision cycles + full-length mocks weekly.
